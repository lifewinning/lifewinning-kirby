Passaic Eternal is an exploration of the physical and intellectual geography of Robert Smithson's essay "A Tour of the Monuments of Passaic", originally published in _Artforum_ in 1976. Our project took the form of a (link:http://passaiceternal.com text:website), an (file: passaiceternal.pdf text: essay), a (link:https://www.youtube.com/watch?feature=player_embedded&v=fDhYnGQE4cs text:video), and (link:https://secure.flickr.com/photos/charlesharlan/sets/72157626185093183/ text:photographs).
